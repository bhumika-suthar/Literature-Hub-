Allama Iqbal

Khudi Ko Kar Buland Itna

Khudi ko kar buland itna ke har taqdeer se pehle,

Khuda bande se khud pooche, bata teri raza kya hai.

Mirza Ghalib

Hazaron Khwahishen Aisi

Hazaron khwahishen aisi ke har khwahish pe dam nikle,

Bohat nikle mere armaan lekin phir bhi kam nikle.

Ahmed Faraz

Ab Ke Hum Bichde

Ab ke hum bichde to shayad kabhi khwabon mein milein,

Jis tarah sookhe hue phool kitaabon mein milein.

Parveen Shakir

Woh Humsafar Tha

Woh humsafar tha magar us se humnawai na thi,

Ke dhoop chaon ka aalam raha judai na thi.

Jaun Elia

Shayad

Shayad mujhe kisi se mohabbat nahi hui,

Lekin yaqeen sab ko dilata raha hoon main.

Amjad Islam Amjad

Barish

Barish ki pehli boonden girne lagi hain,

Abhi bhi waise khushbu aati hai mitti se.

Munir Niazi

Hamesha Dair Kar Deta Hoon Main

Hamesha dair kar deta hoon main,

Har kaam karne mein.

Habib Jalib

Main Nahi Manta

Main nahi manta, main nahi jaanta,

Jo zulum ki rasm hai, main nahi maanta.

Gulzar

Mora Gora Ang Lai Le

Mora gora ang lai le, mohe shyam rang dai de,

Chup jaungi raat hi mein, mohe pehar rang dai de.

Sahir Ludhianvi

Chalo Ek Baar Phir Se

Chalo ek baar phir se ajnabi ban jayen hum dono,

Naa main tum se kuch kahun, naa tum mere kuch kaho.

Nasir Kazmi

Dil Dhundta Hai

Dil dhundta hai phir wahi fursat ke raat din,

Baithen rahe tasavvur-e-jana kiye hue.
